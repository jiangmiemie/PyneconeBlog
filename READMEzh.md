## 使用

### 下载
`pip install -r requirements.txt`


### 检查

check the  `pcconfig.py` and change  `backend` to your backend(port or url)


### 新建
create a pgsql and add  database.ini  , like this :

[postgresql]
host=localhost
database=reflexblog
user=reflexblog
password=***your password***

### 运行

than `bash run.sh`
